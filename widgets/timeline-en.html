1988
Security
First Computer Worm (Morris Worm)
The Morris Worm infected 6,000 UNIX machines, revealing network vulnerabilities and sparking collective awareness.
2001
Security
Code Red and Nimda – Era of Mass Worms
Millions of systems infected within hours. Companies integrate patch management into their processes.
2010
Security
Stuxnet – State-Sponsored Cyberwarfare
The first known malware targeting industrial infrastructure, attributed to a nation-state. Cyberspace becomes a battlefield.
2017
Security
WannaCry – Global Ransomware Attack
150 countries affected, 200,000 machines encrypted. Ransomware becomes the #1 threat to businesses.
2023
AI
Generative AI & New Threats
AI enables voice and visual deepfakes and ultra-personalized phishing, redefining the stakes of digital trust.
2025
Leadership
The CISO Becomes a Business Strategist
Cybersecurity is no longer purely technical: the CISO sits on the executive committee, driving organizational resilience.
